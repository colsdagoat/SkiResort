What can we help you with?

Booking and Reservations

You can book your stay directly through our website. Simply choose your preferred rental, select your dates, and follow the prompts to complete your booking. If you need assistance or prefer to book over the phone, our customer service team is happy to help.

Yes, you can modify your reservation dates based on availability. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible to make changes. Keep in mind that rates may vary based on the new booking dates.

Lodging

Over the Ridge Vacation Rentals offers a variety of lodging options including cabins, cottages, and luxury homes. Each property has unique features and varies in size to accommodate different group sizes and preferences.

Absolutely! Many of our properties are designed with families in mind, offering multiple bedrooms and living spaces. When booking, you can filter options by features like the number of bedrooms to find the perfect fit for your family.

Yes, we do! We understand that pets are part of the family, which is why we offer a selection of pet-friendly accommodations. Please check the property description for the pet policy, including any additional fees or restrictions.

Pricing & Payment

Rental costs are based on several factors including the size of the property, location, and season. Prices may be higher during peak seasons and holidays due to increased demand. All prices are clearly stated on our website when booking.

The price includes your stay at one of our beautiful properties, access to in-house amenities specified in the property description (like a fully equipped kitchen, linens, and Wi-Fi), and cleaning before your arrival. Taxes and any additional fees, such as pet fees if applicable, are also outlined during the booking process.

We strive for transparency in all our pricing. Apart from the rental rate, there may be additional fees for pets (if you're bringing one), a security deposit, or a cleaning fee. These fees will be clearly itemized during the booking process.

Cancellations

Our standard cancellation policy allows for a full refund if canceled at least 30 days before the start of your stay. Cancellations made less than 30 days in advance are subject to partial refunds or credits, depending on the timing. Please refer to our full cancellation policy on our website or contact customer service for more details.

Safety & Accessibility

We aim to accommodate all guests comfortably. Select properties are designed with accessibility in mind. Please consult the property descriptions for specific details or contact our customer service for assistance in finding a suitable rental.
