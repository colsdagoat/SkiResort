Blue Ridge has fun for everyone!

Check out these fun things to do while you stay.

Thrill Seekers' Paradise

Zip lining at Hawksnest Zipline:
Soar through the canopy and enjoy breathtaking views of the Blue Ridge Mountains. Hawksnest Zipline
Whitewater Rafting with High Mountain Expeditions:
Experience adrenaline-pumping rapids on the Watauga or Nolichucky River. High Mountain Expeditions

Leisurely Escapes

Scenic Drive on the Blue Ridge Parkway:
Take a leisurely drive along one of America's most picturesque roads, with numerous overlooks and hiking trails. Blue Ridge Parkway
Fishing at Price Lake:
Relax by the tranquil waters of Price Lake and try your luck at catching trout or bass. Price Lake

Explore Nature's Bounty

Grandfather Mountain Hike:
Embark on an invigorating hike to the summit of Grandfather Mountain, where panoramic views await. Grandfather Mountain
Linville Caverns Adventure:
Delve into the underground world of Linville Caverns and marvel at stunning rock formations. Linville Caverns

Moderate Adventures

Boone Fork Trail Loop:
Enjoy a moderate hike through diverse landscapes, including forests, streams, and meadows. Boone Fork Trail
Kayaking on the New River:
Paddle along the gentle currents of the New River and soak in the tranquility of nature. New River

Savor Local Delights

Farm-to-Table Dining at Vidalia Restaurant:
Indulge in delicious seasonal dishes made from locally sourced ingredients. Vidalia Restaurant
Craft Beer Tasting at Appalachian Mountain Brewery:
Quench your thirst with a variety of craft beers brewed with mountain spring water. Appalachian Mountain Brewery
