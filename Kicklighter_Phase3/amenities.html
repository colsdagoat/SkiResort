Discover Our Amenities

At Over the Ridge Vacation Rentals, we strive to provide the utmost comfort and convenience for our guests. Each of our rental properties comes fully equipped with a range of amenities to enhance your stay in the Blue Ridge Mountains. Here are some of the amenities you can expect to find:

Air Conditioning
Stay cool and comfortable during the warmer months with central air conditioning in all of our properties.
Wi-Fi
Stay connected with complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi available throughout each rental.
Fire pit
Gather around the firepit and roast marshmallows under the starry night sky for a memorable evening with family and friends.
Hot tub
Relax and unwind after a day of exploring with a soothing soak in the hot tub, available in select properties.
Pool
Beat the summer heat with access to a private or shared pool at all of our rental properties.
Kayaks
Take to the water and explore the nearby rivers and lakes with complimentary kayaks available for guest use.
